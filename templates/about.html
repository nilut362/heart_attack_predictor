About The Project




As the saying goes 'Prevention Is Better Than Cure !'

Cardiac Arrests/ Strokes/ Heart Attacks are major killers amongst all all over the globe !

An estimate says that about 10 million people loose their lives because of Heart Attacks each year, worldwide. But what if one already knows when is he gonna get an attack ? The chances of his survival can rise by upto 40%

This changes everything !

The data has been taken from the famous kaggle website. The aim was to successfully generate a model that would be able to comprehend and predict the probablity of a person getting an heart attack which can be of great use in monitoring one's health.
